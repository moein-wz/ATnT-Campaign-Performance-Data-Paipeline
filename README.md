
# 📊 AT&T Campaign Performance Analysis

Analyzed AT&T’s digital advertising campaign across **location, audience, creative formats, and inventory** to identify performance gaps and deliver data-driven recommendations to improve **cost efficiency and engagement**.

---

## 📌 Overview

Digital marketing campaigns generate massive amounts of data across multiple dimensions. However, without structured analysis, it becomes difficult to identify where performance is strong and where budget is being wasted.

In this project, I conducted a **multi-dimensional analysis** of AT&T’s campaign data to uncover key drivers of performance and provide actionable insights for optimization.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Identify **top and bottom performers** across campaign dimensions
* Analyze key metrics: **CPA, CTR, CVR, CPM, Viewability**
* Highlight inefficiencies in targeting, creatives, and placements
* Provide **strategic recommendations** to improve future campaigns

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

* **SQL** – Data cleaning & querying
* **Tableau** – Data visualization & dashboarding
* **Microsoft Excel** – Data preparation & exploration
* **Data Analytics Techniques** – A/B analysis, performance comparison

---

## 📊 Key Insights

### 📍 Location

* **Ohio** consistently performed well across all metrics
* **Arkansas & Kentucky** showed low engagement and high costs
* **Michigan** failed to meet conversion goals

### 👥 Audience

* High-performing segments included:

  * Finance & Home Ownership
  * Travel & Dining
  * B2B and high-income households
* These groups delivered **lower CPA and higher conversion rates**

### 🎨 Creative

* **300×250 banner format** delivered the **lowest CPA** across devices
* Messaging with **clear CTAs and urgency** (“Sign Up Now – 10% Off”) drove highest CTR
* **Desktop users converted better**, while mobile generated more traffic

### 📦 Inventory

* Strong exchanges: **Google Ad Manager, Taboola, OpenX**
* High-performing publishers:

  * Outlook.com (high viewability)
  * ScreenRant (high conversion rate)
  * MSN (large audience reach)

---

## 💡 Recommendations

* **Scale high-performing creatives** (300×250 format)
* Use **CTA-driven, urgency-based messaging** to increase engagement
* Allocate:

  * **Desktop → conversions**
  * **Mobile → awareness & reach**
* Invest more in **high-value audience segments**
* Combine **programmatic + direct buys** for better performance

---

## 📈 Business Impact

* Identified opportunities to **reduce CPA** through better creative and targeting strategies
* Improved understanding of **high-performing audience segments**
* Provided a clear roadmap to **optimize campaign spend and engagement**
